# Gunnin

**Status**: In Production  
**Key**: C Min
**BPM**: 170 (85)

## Overview

Formerly "Big Bags," written Nov 2024 during initial writing and recording sessions. Re-written July 2025 as stream-of-consciousness free verses written against backdrop of ongoing Israeli genocidal campaign in Gaza, colonization of Palestine, and joint air strikes with USA on Iran. Featuring vocal sample of Big Boi "Iraq, how's that, now he's gunnin for Iran" from Big Boi feat. Andre 3000 & Raekwon "Royal Flush."
